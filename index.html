About Us
We are a cover band founded in 1981. We play real rock and roll from the 60s through the 2000s.

Our material includes songs from the following artists:

  • David Bowie
  • The Beatles
  • The Rolling Stones
  • Elvis Costello
  • The Doors
  • Devo
  • The Clash
  • And More!
